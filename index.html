Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this AI Engineer SDLC program meant for?

This program is for developers, data scientists, or tech enthusiasts who want to master the full AI development lifecycle — from design to deployment in real-world settings.

What technologies and tools will I use?

You’ll work with industry-standard tools: Python, ML frameworks (TensorFlow / PyTorch), Docker, Kubernetes, AWS/GCP, CI/CD pipelines, version control, MLOps tooling, and more.

Are live projects and practicals included?

Yes — 100% of the curriculum involves hands-on projects, real-time debugging, and deploying AI systems under mentor supervision.

Do I need prior machine learning experience?

Some familiarity with programming (especially Python) and basic ML concepts helps. But we begin from fundamentals and support all learners to catch up.

Will I receive job-ready skills & certification?

Absolutely. On completion, you’ll have real projects, a polished portfolio, and a certificate that demonstrates your readiness to work in AI engineering roles.
